Previously, I was lucky to have the opportunities to work with Raquel Urtasun at Uber ATG and Min Sun at National Tsing Hua University. In addition to my research experiences, I build interesting open-source projects such as DeepLearningFlappyBird, which has been featured on Hacker News.
