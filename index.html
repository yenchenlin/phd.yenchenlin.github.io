Bio. I am a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL, under the supervision of Phillip Isola. I am interested in robotics.

Previously, I was lucky to have the opportunities to work with Raquel Urtasun at Uber ATG, Min Sun at National Tsing Hua University, Ming-Yu Liu at NVIDIA, and Jia-Bin Huang at Virginia Tech. In addition to my research experiences, I build interesting open-source projects such as DeepLearningFlappyBird, which has been featured on Hacker News.

Real Forest
Real Forest is a feature I developed for well-known iOS app - Forest: Stay focused, be present when I was an intern there. This feature let user plant a real tree in the world using the coins they earned in the app. So far, it has helped planting 8000+ real trees in India and Zambia.
